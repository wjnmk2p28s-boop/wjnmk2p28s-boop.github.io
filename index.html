Felix von Castell
Coding Advanced, Technische Hochschule Nuremberg, Faculty Design

Abstract

AI Image generation is available for every one that owns a smartphone or a computer. Results often feel completely random even though they can be controlled by certain Settings like Steps in the Denoising process, classifier guidance scale and Seed. Additional options like Low Rand Adaptation Models or ControlNets give the user more control over the output. This exact feeling of control over the output of the image genereation is the goal of this application.
