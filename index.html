Abstract

AI Image generation is available for every one that owns a smartphone or a computer. Results often feel completely random even though they can be controlled by certain Settings like Steps in the Denoising process, classifier guidance scale and Seed. Additional options like Low Rand Adaptation Models or ControlNets give the user more control over the output.

Results

Showcase your experimental results, data visualizations, or generated samples. Next to a classic generation method using the model itself, an added self trained LoRa impacts the generation and makes more precise results possible. One of the two LoRas is the "Saltlake LoRa" that was trained with a Dataset of a Saltlake in Utah. Without LoRa no typical features attributes visible in image they become evident once the LoRa is applied.

You can use the clean grid below to display multiple images side-by-side. The second LoRa, "Denim Jeans LoRa", aims to enhance the texture and realism of denim garments in generated images, which can often look flat or overly smooth. The comparisons below highlight the effect of this LoRa, where the denim jeans generated without LoRa lack specific texture, making them look less realistic. Once the LoRa is applied, the details and folds in the fabric become more clear, significantly improving the realism and visual fidelity of the denim clothing.
